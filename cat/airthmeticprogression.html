Airthemetic Progression

Lets take a simple airthmetic progression 1 + 3 + 5 + 7

The formula to find sum of airthmetic progression is

number of airthmatic progression is 4 

                    
   sum of airthmatic progression= n/2(first airthmatic progression number + last airthmatic progression number)
   sum = 4/2(1+7) = 16

calculating number of airthmetic progression if first and last airthmatic progression and the difference between each airthmatic progression is given

lets take a complex airthmatic progression 1 + 6 + 11 .... + 101

Here we don't know number of airthmatic progression but we know the first and the last airthmatic progression and the difference between each airthmatic progression. difference is 5 and the first A.P is 1 and last A.P is 101 

                    
    an = a1 + (n - 1) * d;
    101 = 1 + (n-1) * 5
    100/5 = n-1
    21 = n

an airthmatic progression 1 + 6 + 11 + . . .

In this case we don't know the last or any airthmatic progression after 11 .lets say we want to calculate the 7th Airthmatic progression

we use above formula

                    
    a7 = a1 + (n - 1) * d;
    a7 = 1 + (7-1) * 5
    a7 = 31

Another formula to calculate A.P 

                    
    sum of an = n / 2(2 * a1 + (n - 1) * d)

n , a1 and d is known and then this formula will work
