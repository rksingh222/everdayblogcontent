Geometric Progression

Lets take a geometric progression 1 + 2 + 4 + 8 + ...

This geometric progression can be written in this form 20 * 1 + 21 * 1 + 22 * 1 + 23 * 1 + ....

This geometric progression is basically in the form a * r0 + a * r1 + a * r2 + a * r3

                    
    sum of g.p = a * (rn - 1)/(r - 1);
    nth g.p or xn = a * rn-1;

lets take a G.P 1 + 3 + 9 + 27 + .....

finding the nth G.P of this number is quite easy as we know the formula and r and a values

here r is 3 and a is 1. Lets suppose we want to find 6th G.P of given series. Hence n will be 5

                    
    x6 = a * r5;
    x6 = 1 * 35;
    x6 = 81;

suppose we want to calculate sum of G.P of 1 + 3 + 9 + .... till n = 6 means nth G.P

we have r = 3 , n = 6 and a = 1 . lets calculate G.P as we have the formula

we use above formula

                    
    sum of g.p = a * (rn - 1)/(r - 1);
    sum of g.p = 1 * (36 - 1)/(3 - 1);
    sum of g.p = 728/2 = 364

we can use these formulas to calculate g.p
