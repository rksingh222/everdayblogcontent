My story of visit in bicycle to weribee south beach approximately 18km

On 23rd of October 2022 i was very curious to visit a beach because i spend most of my time staying in the house in Melbourne. First of all last five years i was in bit of dilemma and my health deteriorated somehow i got a chance to travel to melbourne and i decided to visit this werribee south beach first.I am thankful to the people who brought me here.

I don't have money for now i am just penniless and i have to borrow money from people which is not working. so i decided to visit the beach by bicycle. Sometimes i feel if i had money and I was independent then things would have been different. I do not need to take someones order. Someone brainwashed me and did not give me idea that once i have a job in my bucket i should not give up and should not do further studies. Anyways it taught me something but i need money and for now lets go further in the story. I borrowed the cycle and it was my first visit so i was doubtful in one hand but in the other hand i wanted to visit the beach. So i did not think twice i just took the cycle and rode towards the tarneit road but in the middle i got distracted and fell from cycle. I was injured this was not my first time it happened before also. I am quite used to it. I did not get distracted. I continued my travelling. I began from the point i fell. The cycle chain got out from its position and i had to fix it again. I saw a shopping store just right of me and it had around 15 stores such as My Clinic, Roll & Roast Made Easy, Weribee Bakery, laundry, chemist, SIMS Liquor, Barber Ambitious Hair Salon, Golden place Chinese Restaurant.

Ok i got up. I got bruises in my right leg and i had bit of blood and my pant got dirty a bit but it was just in the middle of my right part of pant. I felt nervous but i got up and started moving towards edward road. i found some native people from australia and i shook my hand towards them. I was happy that my cycle is still working because after falling it was in critical condition. I just thought that i have to go because my life is not so important after all. I have already been in critical condition by falling or in some other means. I have fallen many times but getting up again was very difficult. But some people helped me many times to come out of this. Then I reached wattle avenue and edwards Road. Reaching to Duncans road my google map was giving me a location but it had three roads and all of them were going in different direction. So i stopped near the duncan road while the bridge was near. I asked some people they were not ready to tell me i had to wait for someone because every one was driving a car and the mirrors were closed. I waited for some to respond. Atlast i found a person driving car with his girlfriend or may be wife. He told me to follow the bridge. I followed the duncan bridge and somehow still it was confusing. When i finished travelling the bridge it had three ways one was to my left and to my right and straight. I decided to go right but when i tried to find it from google map it was giving me wrong direction. So i better felt to ask from a lady who was driving car as she stopped when the traffic lights had a red signal she was chinese by look but she did not respond. Then there was another lady who also stopped she told me to go straight . I moved straight and while i was riding cycle i was tired as it was bit sunny and it was a upward stream. the location when i was travelling after the bridge was a farming laund and had no houses. I was bit sceptical whether i will reach. And then after travelling 6 km more i found the beach. When i reached the beach i asked a native to take my picture.He took my picture As it was around 5:00 pm i had a plan not to stay more because i had to travel 18km back to reach and take a rest as i was already tired. I saw a girl who were enjoying in their car near the beach when i was there. Also i saw some more people there. However the it was not crowded as its winter and the water is pretty cold. I took a turn and started travelling back to bombala street with the same road. And i reached it was a tiresome journey. My advice if you are travelling to this beach don't go on a cycle because it does not have a cycle path for around 10km. Have a nice day take care

Visit of WilliamsTown Beach - 31 October 2022

I decided to visit the beach on Monday. I saw the google map it showed sayers road and i thought sayers road is the one which is near the Davis Road completion before the gurudwara which comes near the bridge after Davis Road. I visited Davis Road and took a turn to Leakes Road and i felt it is sayers road. I decided to go to Bunnings and fix my cycle because the cycle tires are not rotating properly. I went to bunnings and asked a man to see and fix my cycle he told we can't fix it but i can see your issue. I told him to sit on the cycle and find the problem but he denied and he was angry with me. I didnt like the way he behaved . Then i started looking for mosquito net but i didnt find that in bunnings. Opposite bunnings it has kmart i checked in that also. it had no mosquito net. I took my cycle inside and no one made objection. I started my travel for a beach and in the middle i met a pakistani guy. he showed me the map and map showed if i continue walking on the same road for around 10km i will find geelong highway and there with that help i can reach to williamstown.

It had cycle roots. On the way i found a cycle shop i thought to repair my cycle but it was a electric cycle store . After a while i had to walk instead of cycling because it had no path for cycling . After a while i found a place to start the cycling and i continued cycling for around an hour. Before reaching to williamstown beach i saw people wearing horror dress because its a halloween. I saw a woolsworth and a tennis court while travelling towards victoria street. I went towards victoria street and few minutes later i was on the beach. I locked my cycle and removed all my clothes and started moving towards the beach to take path. It was so cold. I took few minutes to get wet and then i decided to move back. It started raining and two small puppies started barking. I touched them and wore my clothes and was ready to move back for my house. I took a chocolate for Jazz and it took hours to reach my house. I was tired and helpless. I had 17 dollars which i was not ready to spend. After a while i reached Home back safely but tired. I was hungry. I had a coffee and watched a cricket match and then went to sound sleep.It was a nice beach. I didn't find many people in the beach just few of them.
